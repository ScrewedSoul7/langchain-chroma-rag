# Comprehensive Guide to Devil Fruits

In the world of One Piece, Devil Fruits grant extraordinary abilities at the cost of the user's ability to swim. Exposure to seawater or Seastone nullifies their powers, and advanced Haki can be used to bypass their defenses.

There are three primary categories of Devil Fruits:
* **Paramecia**: Grants superhuman abilities or environment manipulation.
* **Zoan**: Allows transformation into an animal or animal-hybrid form, heavily boosting physical stats.
* **Logia**: Allows the user to become, produce, and completely control a natural element, making them intangible.

---

## Paramecia-Type Devil Fruits

* **Gomu Gomu no Mi (Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Nika)**
    * **Power**: Turns the body into rubber, granting immense elasticity, blunt force resistance, and immunity to lightning. (Note: Later revealed to possess Mythical Zoan properties).
    * **Notable User**: Monkey D. Luffy

* **Ope Ope no Mi**
    * **Power**: Known as the "Ultimate Operation Fruit." Creates a spherical space ("Room") where the user can manipulate objects and people with absolute surgical precision, swap locations, and switch personalities.
    * **Notable User**: Trafalgar D. Water Law

* **Doru Doru no Mi**
    * **Power**: Produces and controls sticky, heavy wax that can harden into steel-like constructs or traps.
    * **Notable User**: Mr. 3 (Galdino)

* **Bara Bara no Mi**
    * **Power**: Allows the user to split their body into pieces and control them independently in the air, making them completely immune to slashing attacks.
    * **Notable User**: Buggy the Clown

* **Hana Hana no Mi**
    * **Power**: Allows the user to sprout multiple copies of their body parts (limbs, eyes, ears) on any surface within their line of sight.
    * **Notable User**: Nico Robin

* **Ito Ito no Mi**
    * **Power**: Generates and manipulates incredibly sharp, durable, and nearly invisible strings capable of cutting objects or controlling people like puppets.
    * **Notable User**: Donquixote Doflamingo

* **Gura Gura no Mi**
    * **Power**: Generates massive shockwaves and vibrations through air, land, or sea, capable of creating powerful earthquakes and tsunamis.
    * **Notable User**: Edward Newgate (Whitebeard)

* **Doku Doku no Mi**
    * **Power**: Generates and controls various types of highly lethal poison and venom from the body.
    * **Notable User**: Magellan

* **Bari Bari no Mi**
    * **Power**: Creates completely indestructible, invisible-to-semi-translucent barriers by crossing fingers.
    * **Notable User**: Bartolomeo

* **Mochi Mochi no Mi**
    * **Power**: Turns the body into thick, sticky mochi, allowing for extreme shape-shifting, stretching, and capturing enemies.
    * **Notable User**: Charlotte Katakuri

* **Bomu Bomu no Mi**
    * **Power**: Makes any part of the user's body (including breath and secretions) explosive, granting immunity to explosions.
    * **Notable User**: Mr. 5

* **Kilo Kilo no Mi**
    * **Power**: Allows the user to change their physical weight at will, ranging from 1 kilogram to 10,000 kilograms.
    * **Notable User**: Miss Valentine

* **Kage Kage no Mi**
    * **Power**: Manifests and controls shadows, enabling the user to steal shadows from others to build a zombie army or ingest them to boost their own strength.
    * **Notable User**: Gecko Moria

* **Noro Noro no Mi**
    * **Power**: Emits microscopic "Noro Photon" beams that slow down any object or living being for exactly 30 seconds.
    * **Notable User**: Foxy

* **Soru Soru no Mi**
    * **Power**: Extracts and manipulates the souls of living beings, allowing the user to shorten a person's lifespan or infuse souls into inanimate objects to bring them to life.
    * **Notable User**: Big Mom (Charlotte Linlin)

## Zoan-Type Devil Fruits

* **Ushi Ushi no Mi, Model: Bison**
    * **Power**: Transforms the user into a full bison or a massive human-bison hybrid, vastly increasing physical strength and charging power.
    * **Notable User**: Dalton

* **Tori Tori no Mi, Model: Falcon**
    * **Power**: Transforms the user into a falcon or human-falcon hybrid, granting high-speed aerial flight and enhanced vision.
    * **Notable User**: Pell

* **Hito Hito no Mi**
    * **Power**: Grants a non-human creature human attributes, including high level human intelligence, speech, and medical understanding.
    * **Notable User**: Tony Tony Chopper

* **Ryu Ryu no Mi, Model: Pteranodon**
    * **Power**: An Ancient Zoan that transforms the user into a pteranodon, granting high-speed flight, extreme agility, and immense physical durability.
    * **Notable User**: King the Conflagration

* **Neko Neko no Mi, Model: Saber-Toothed Tiger**
    * **Power**: An Ancient Zoan that grants the predatory instincts, massive fangs, and physical power of a saber-toothed tiger.
    * **Notable User**: Who's-Who

### Mythical Zoan Sub-Types
* **Tori Tori no Mi, Model: Phoenix**
    * **Power**: Transforms the user into a legendary blue-flame phoenix, granting the ability of flight and instantaneous healing via "Flames of Resurrection."
    * **Notable User**: Marco

* **Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Daibutsu**
    * **Power**: Transforms the user into a giant golden Buddha statue, granting massive physical size, strength, and the ability to launch golden energy shockwaves.
    * **Notable User**: Sengoku

* **Uo Uo no Mi, Model: Seiryu**
    * **Power**: Transforms the user into a massive azure eastern dragon, granting flight, scale-durability, and powerful elemental breath attacks (like fire blasts).
    * **Notable User**: Kaido

* **Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Kitsune**
    * **Power**: Transforms the user into a mythical nine-tailed fox, granting the ability to cast advanced illusions and shapeshift into exact copies of other people.
    * **Notable User**: Catarína Devon

* **Hito Hito no Mi, Model: Onyudo**
    * **Power**: Transforms the user into a giant spiritual warrior monk (Oni-like entity).
    * **Notable User**: Onimaru

## Logia-Type Devil Fruits

* **Yami Yami no Mi**
    * **Power**: Controls darkness and gravity. While the user cannot become intangible like other Logia types, they can absorb physical damage and temporarily nullify the Devil Fruit powers of anyone they touch.
    * **Notable User**: Marshall D. Teach (Blackbeard)

* **Hie Hie no Mi**
    * **Power**: Controls, generates, and turns the body into pure ice, allowing the user to freeze environments, oceans, and targets instantly.
    * **Notable User**: Kuzan (Aokiji)

* **Suna Suna no Mi**
    * **Power**: Controls, generates, and transforms the user's body into sand. Grants the power to create sandstorms and dehydrate objects or living beings upon physical touch.
    * **Notable User**: Crocodile

* **Pika Pika no Mi**
    * **Power**: Controls, generates, and transforms the body into light. Enables the user to travel at the speed of light and emit highly destructive laser beams.
    * **Notable User**: Borsalino (Kizaru)

* **Mera Mera no Mi**
    * **Power**: Controls, generates, and transforms the body into fire, enabling devastating heat and flame-based attacks.
    * **Notable User**: Portgas D. Ace (formerly), Sabo (currently)

* **Goro Goro no Mi**
    * **Power**: Controls, generates, and transforms the body into lightning, granting extreme movement speed and immense electrical destructive output.
    * **Notable User**: Enel
